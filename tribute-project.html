Healthcare Workers

The Heroes of the Corona Virus Pandemic

When COVID first arose, people were afraid of its threat, but these brave heroes fought the virus head-on to save as many lives as they can. Healthcare Workers were the frontline during this tragic Pandemic that took many lives of people all around the world. I want all of us to take time to appreciate all their efforts during 2020.
